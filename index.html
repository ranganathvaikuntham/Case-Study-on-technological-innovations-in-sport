Wearable tech et IOT

Wearable technology is a hot market in recent times. With the purchase of Fitbit by Google for a whopping 5 Billion, more attention is being drawn to this domain than ever before. Research advancements in IOT - Sensor Networks, Analytics are revolutionilising the domain of Wearable technologies and their connectivity with the internet.

The market for Wearable technology is vast and ever growing, but for our research let us stick to their usage and applications in the sports industry.

First the basics
What is IOT and Wearable Tech?
IOT or internet of things represents networks of variety of connected objects. These objects are capable of connecting and sharing information with the internet. Wearable technology are simply appliacances or devices equipped with IOT technologies and are for being worn by humans.
